# 8. Phase Closure – Go-Live Readiness Confirmation

## 8.1 Objective
Formally close Phase 4 by confirming that the organization is fully prepared for Go-Live and that all deliverables, risks, and technical prerequisites have been validated.

## 8.2 Final Readiness Review

### Technical & Functional Readiness
- Cutover steps validated and rehearsed  
- All systems aligned between pre-prod and production  
- Monitoring dashboards configured and tested  
- Backups and rollback procedures validated  

### Operational Readiness
- Support teams fully trained  
- Hypercare staffing confirmed  
- Escalation matrix tested and approved  
- Incident playbook finalized  

### Risk Readiness
- No critical open risks  
- Mitigation plans implemented  
- Rollback triggers understood by all teams  
- Business continuity confirmed  

### Communication Readiness
- Go-Live communications prepared and approved  
- Distribution channels scheduled  
- Stakeholders aligned  

## 8.3 Outstanding Items Review
- Remaining minor issues documented  
- Ownership and timelines assigned  
- No remaining blockers for Go-Live  

## 8.4 Sign-Off & Transition
The phase officially closes once the following have been completed:
- Readiness validation approved  
- Stakeholders sign-off  
- Transition to **Phase 5 — Go-Live Execution & Hypercare** authorized  

## 8.5 Deliverables
- Final readiness report  
- Sign-off sheet  
- Open items tracker  
- Phase closure summary
