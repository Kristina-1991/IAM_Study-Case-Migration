# 6. Hypercare Support Plan

## Objective
Provide enhanced support during the first weeks after Go-Live to ensure stability, fix issues quickly, and secure user adoption.

## Hypercare Duration
Typically 2â€“4 weeks depending on complexity and risk profile.

## Support Structure
- Daily or semi-daily checkpoints  
- Dedicated IAM experts available on-call  
- Priority incident queue  
- Direct channels with application owners  

## Monitoring & Reporting
- Authentication success rate tracking  
- Provisioning queue health  
- Error trend analysis  
- Weekly hypercare reports  

## Exit Criteria
- No critical or major incidents in the last 7 days  
- Stabilized provisioning  
- CA and SSO policies behaving as expected  
- Operational teams fully autonomous  

## Deliverables
- Hypercare runbook  
- Metrics dashboard  
- Transition-to-BAU checklist
