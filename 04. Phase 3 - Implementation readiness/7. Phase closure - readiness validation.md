## 7.1 Objective
Confirm that all implementation prerequisites have been completed and validated, ensuring that the project can safely transition to Go-Live preparation.

## 7.2 Scope of the Readiness Review
- Validation of technical readiness (infrastructure, integrations, identities, policies)
- Verification of configuration completeness in Entra ID & Identity Governance
- Data readiness: identity sources, attribute mapping, HRIS alignment
- Business readiness: communication, support preparation, training
- Security & compliance readiness: access policies, SoD, audit requirements
- Confirmation that all efforts in Phase 3 meet quality and completeness criteria

## 7.3 Readiness Checklist

### Technical Readiness
- All configurations for Entra ID, Conditional Access, Identity Governance completed  
- Connectors to HRIS, AD, and business applications validated  
- UAT environment fully aligned with production  
- Logs and monitoring dashboards configured in Sentinel  

### Data Readiness
- Identity data quality validated  
- Attribute reconciliation rules approved  
- Role and entitlement mappings finalized  

### Business & Operational Readiness
- Support model validated  
- Incident and escalation workflows defined  
- Training delivered to administrators and support teams  

### Risk & Compliance Readiness
- Residual risks reviewed and assigned  
- SoD violations resolved or documented  
- All audit-relevant controls implemented  

## 7.4 Readiness Criteria
- No critical risks or blockers remaining  
- All mandatory deliverables have been approved  
- Stakeholders confirm readiness for Phase 4  
- A clear Go/No-Go recommendation is issued  

## 7.5 Sign-Off
A governance meeting captures the formal validation and sign-off to close Phase 3 and initiate Go-Live preparation.
