# 3. Business Requirements Matrix
## 3.1 Overview

Business requirements were collected through stakeholder interviews, security assessments, and process walkthroughs. 
They aim to enhance lifecycle automation, reduce access-related risks, and establish a scalable IAM foundation aligned with regulatory expectations.

## 3.2 Business requirements summary matrix 

| **ID** | **Requirement**             | **Description**                                                            | **Priority** | **Owner**              | **Dependencies**  |
| ------ | --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------ | ---------------------- | ----------------- |
| BR-01  | Centralized Identity Store  | Consolidate identities into Entra ID as the authoritative identity source. | High         | IAM Lead               | HRIS, AD          |
| BR-02  | Automated JML               | Implement automated Joiner–Mover–Leaver workflows with HR integration.     | High         | HR + IAM               | HR System         |
| BR-03  | Access Catalog              | Create a standardized access catalog for all roles & entitlements.         | Medium       | Security Governance    | App Owners        |
| BR-04  | Streamlined Access Requests | Provide a single enterprise access request portal with approval workflows. | High         | Business Units         | Access Catalog    |
| BR-05  | Periodic Certifications     | Automate access reviews and provide auditable results.                     | High         | Risk & Compliance      | App Integrations  |
| BR-06  | Privileged Access Control   | Strengthen governance of admin and elevated accounts.                      | High         | CISO Office            | IT Ops            |
| BR-07  | Logging & Monitoring        | Integrate identity logs with Sentinel for detection and response.          | Medium       | SOC                    | IAM Ops           |
| BR-08  | Zero Trust Adoption         | Implement MFA, conditional access, and identity-based segmentation.        | Medium       | Security Architecture  | Entra ID Policies |
| BR-09  | Contractor Access Framework | Standardize identity lifecycle for external users.                         | Medium       | Procurement + Business | HR / App Owners   |
| BR-10  | Compliance Evidence         | Improve audit readiness with consistent, centralized evidence.             | High         | Risk & Compliance      | IAM Governance    |
