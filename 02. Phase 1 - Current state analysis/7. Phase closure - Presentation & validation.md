# 7. Phase Closure — Presentation & Validation
## 7.1 Objective

Validate the findings of the Current State Analysis, the business requirements, and the identified gaps before starting the definition of the Target Architecture and migration roadmap.

## 7.2 Deliverables

- Current State Analysis deck
- Business Requirements Summary
- Gap Analysis matrix
- Risk Register & prioritization heatmap
- Updated stakeholder map & RACI
- Meeting minutes and validation comments

## 7.3 Validation Activities

- Steering Committee presentation (CISO, CIO, Business Sponsors)
- Workshop with IAM team & application owners
- Review by Risk, Compliance & Architecture teams
- Alignment on scope, risks, and priorities
- Formal sign-off (email or Confluence workflow approval)

## 7.4 Acceptance Criteria

- All gaps validated or updated by stakeholders
- No unresolved high-severity blocker
- All risks accepted or assigned owners
- Business requirements validated at 80–90% completeness
- Agreement to proceed to **Phase 2: Future State Definition**
