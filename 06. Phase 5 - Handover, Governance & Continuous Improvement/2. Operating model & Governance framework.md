# 2. Operating Model & Governance Framework

## 2.1 Objective
Define the long-term operational governance of the IAM ecosystem, ensuring accountability, continuous improvement, and compliance across business and IT.

## 2.2 Operating Model Principles
- **Centralized governance**, decentralized execution  
- **Security-by-design** for all new applications and processes  
- **Role-based accountability** across HR, IT, Security, and Business Units  
- **Audit-ready operations** through defined reviews and metrics  

## 2.3 Core Governance Structures

### IAM Steering Committee
- Quarterly meetings  
- Validates major roadmap decisions  
- Ensures alignment with enterprise strategy  

### IAM Operational Committee
- Monthly meetings  
- Oversees operations, incidents, and continuous improvement  
- Tracks KPIs, audit findings, and access review cycles  

### Architecture Review Board
- Ensures adherence to IAM standards  
- Validates integrations and new applications  

## 2.4 Governance Artifacts
- IAM policies  
- Standards & guidelines  
- Process documentation  
- RACI matrices  
- Audit logs and compliance reports
