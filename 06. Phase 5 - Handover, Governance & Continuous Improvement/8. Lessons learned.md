# 8. Lessons Learned

## 8.1 Objective
Capture insights to improve future IAM initiatives and strengthen governance.

## 8.2 Key Lessons

### What Worked Well
- Early alignment between HR, IT, and Security  
- Strong governance cadence  
- Automation reduced operational workload significantly  
- Sentinel monitoring improved visibility  

### What Could Be Improved
- Data quality required more upfront time  
- Some business units needed additional change management support  
- Role definitions and access models should be clarified earlier  

### Recommendations
- Start future projects with data cleansing and HR engagement  
- Formalize access governance roles before design workshops  
- Increase training efforts for application owners
