# 6. Continuous Improvement Plan

## 6.1 Purpose
Ensure that the IAM ecosystem evolves through iterative improvements, risk mitigation, and compliance updates.

## 6.2 Improvement Drivers
- New regulatory requirements (GDPR, NIS2, DORA)  
- New business applications or M&A activities  
- Audit findings and incident root causes  
- Changes in workforce or organizational structure  

## 6.3 Continuous Improvement Activities
- Annual IAM roadmap update  
- Quarterly policy review  
- Monthly KPIs and incident analysis  
- Regular lifecycle automation enhancements  
- Application onboarding standardization  

## 6.4 Continuous Feedback Loop
1. Detect (Monitoring, KPIs, Sentinel alerts)  
2. Analyze (Incident reviews, stakeholder feedback)  
3. Improve (Process redesign, automation)  
4. Validate (Testing, governance approval)
