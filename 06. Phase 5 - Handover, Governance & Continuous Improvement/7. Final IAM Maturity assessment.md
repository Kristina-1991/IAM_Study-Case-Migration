# 7. Final IAM Maturity Assessment

## 7.1 Objective
Evaluate the organization’s maturity at project closure using a standardized IAM maturity model.

## 7.2 Domains Assessed
- Identity Lifecycle Management  
- Authentication & Access Management  
- Access Governance  
- Privileged Access (Scoping)  
- Security Monitoring & Logging  
- Compliance & Audit Readiness  
- Operational Governance  

## 7.3 Maturity Scoring Model (0–5)
- 0 – Nonexistent  
- 1 – Initial / Ad Hoc  
- 2 – Repeatable  
- 3 – Defined & Standardized  
- 4 – Managed & Automated  
- 5 – Optimized & Predictive  

## 7.4 Expected Outcome (Post-Project)
- Identity lifecycle: **4 – Automated**  
- Entra ID Governance: **3.5 – Standardized with automation**  
- Security monitoring: **4 – Sentinel-enabled detection**  
- Processes & SOPs: **3 – Defined**  
- Overall maturity: **3.6 / 5**
