## 1.1 Purpose
This meeting marks the beginning of the transition from the implementation team to the operational teams.  
It ensures that ownership of the IAM solution, processes, and governance is fully transferred and understood.

## 1.2 Attendees
- IAM Lead / Program Manager  
- Security Office (CISO / ISO)  
- IT Operations  
- HRIS / Application Owners  
- Service Desk & Support Teams  
- Architecture Team  

## 1.3 Agenda
1. Summary of project achievements and delivered scope  
2. Review of the operating model and governance framework  
3. Review of IAM procedures & SOPs  
4. Presentation of new roles and responsibilities  
5. Handover of operational documentation  
6. Open risks, issues, and mitigations  
7. Next steps and transition timeline  

## 1.4 Expected Outputs
- Handover checklist approved  
- Ownership confirmed for all operational activities  
- Start of operational governance cadences  
- Acceptance of documentation & access rights
