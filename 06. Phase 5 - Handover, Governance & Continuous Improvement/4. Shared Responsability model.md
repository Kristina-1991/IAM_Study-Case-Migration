# 4. Shared Responsibility Model (Business vs IT)

## 4.1 Objective
Define clear and sustainable responsibilities across Business, HR, Security,IT Operations and the Cloud Provider (Microsoft Entra ID).

## 4.2 Key Principles
- Business owns **access decisions**  and validates entitlements.
- IT owns **technical implementation** and operational monitoring. 
- Security defines **policies & controls** and ensures compliance.
- HR owns **identity source accuracy** and lifecycle triggers.
- Provider (Microsoft) owns **platform security, availability and compliance certifications.**

## 4.3 Shared Responsibility Matrix

### Business Units
- Define job roles & required access  
- Approve access requests  
- Perform access reviews  

### HR
- Maintain correct identity data  
- Trigger joiner/mover/leaver events  

### IT / IAM Operations
- Implement provisioning & governance workflows. 
- Maintain integration flows.
- Monitor anomalies & respond to incidents. 

### Security Office
- Define policies (MFA, Conditional Access, SoD)  
- Validate compliance with regulations  
- Oversee risk management  

### Audit & Compliance
- Conduct periodic audits  
- Validate evidence and logs  

### Cloud Provider (Microsoft Entra ID) 
- Ensure platform availability
- Maintain infrasturcture security (datacenter, network,OS).
- Provide compliance certifications (ISO, SOC, GDPR alignment).
- Deliver product updates, patches and feature enhancements.
- Guarantee baseline logging and monitoring capabilities.
