# 4. Shared Responsibility Model (Business vs IT)

## 4.1 Objective
Define clear and sustainable responsibilities across Business, HR, Security, and IT Operations.

## 4.2 Key Principles
- Business owns **access decisions**  
- IT owns **technical implementation**  
- Security defines **policies & controls**  
- HR owns **identity source accuracy**  

## 4.3 Shared Responsibility Matrix

### Business Units
- Define job roles & required access  
- Approve access requests  
- Perform access reviews  

### HR
- Maintain correct identity data  
- Trigger joiner/mover/leaver events  

### IT / IAM Operations
- Implement provisioning & governance  
- Maintain integration flows  
- Monitor anomalies & respond to incidents  

### Security Office
- Define policies (MFA, Conditional Access, SoD)  
- Validate compliance with regulations  
- Oversee risk management  

### Audit & Compliance
- Conduct periodic audits  
- Validate evidence and logs
