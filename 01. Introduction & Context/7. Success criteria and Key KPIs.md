## Success Criteria & Key KPIs

The success of the IAM transformation is measured across **strategic, operational, and compliance dimensions**, ensuring tangible business value and sustainable governance.

### **Success Criteria**
- Modernized IAM environment fully migrated to Microsoft Entra ID.  
- Automation of identity lifecycle (Joiner–Mover–Leaver) with measurable efficiency gains.  
- Robust access governance with formalized IAM CoE and Shared Responsibility Model.  
- Alignment with regulatory standards (NIS2, GDPR, ISO 27001) with auditable evidence.  
- Improved stakeholder confidence and adoption of IAM processes.

### **Key KPIs**
| KPI                                      | Target / Metric                                         |
|------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| Provisioning SLA                           | < 24 hours                                             |
| Offboarding SLA                            | < 1 hour                                               |
| Incident Response SLA                      | < 4 hours                                              |
| Access Review Completion Rate              | 100% within defined period                             |
| Privileged Account Risk Remediation        | 95% remediated within defined timeframe                |
| Compliance Audit Findings                  | Zero critical findings related to IAM                  |
| Automation Coverage                        | > 90% of identity lifecycle processes automated       |
| User Satisfaction / Stakeholder Adoption  | > 85% adoption rate for new IAM processes             |
| Governance Adherence                        | 100% compliance with IAM CoE policies and SLAs        |

> **Note:** KPIs are tracked via Entra ID reporting dashboards, ensuring transparency, auditable evidence, and continuous improvement.
